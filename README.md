# Hoyt Sayler – Mathematics, Research, and Philosophy

Welcome to my personal website repository! This repository contains all the files and content for my portfolio website, which showcases my research, essays, and work at the intersection of mathematics, philosophy, and social theory.

## About Me
I am an undergraduate student pursuing a career as a mathematics professor and writer. My research interests focus on using **mathematical modeling to analyze social systems and socialist theory**. I aim to connect rigorous quantitative analysis with political and social philosophy.
